The Julia Language Slack

The Julia Language has an active Slack workspace/community with over 7,000 members. Slack is a space to have quick and informal correspondence with others in the community. We ask that if you have Julia usage questions you post them on Discourse so others can benefit from them or on Stack Overflow (but the Discourse community is much more welcoming in our opinion).
